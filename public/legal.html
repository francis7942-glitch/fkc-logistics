FKC Logistics ("we", "our", or "us") is operated by FNC Nathaniel's Food Corporation, a company based in City of San Fernando, Pampanga, Philippines. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use the FKC Logistics cold storage management application.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Account information: Name, username, and role when creating user accounts within the application.
  • Business data: Client information, inventory records, transaction logs, billing data, and spare parts information entered by authorized users.
  • QuickBooks integration data: OAuth tokens used to connect to your QuickBooks Online account. We store these securely in our database to enable invoice creation on your behalf.
  • Usage data: Basic application usage patterns to improve performance and functionality.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information solely to:

  • Provide cold storage management services including inventory tracking, billing, and transaction logging
  • Create invoices in your connected QuickBooks Online account when you request it
  • Authenticate users and maintain session security
  • Improve and maintain the application

3. Data Storage

All data is stored securely using Supabase (PostgreSQL), hosted in Singapore (Southeast Asia region). Data is encrypted at rest and in transit using industry-standard TLS encryption.

4. QuickBooks Integration

When you connect your QuickBooks Online account, we store OAuth access and refresh tokens in our secure database. These tokens are used exclusively to create invoices on your behalf. We do not access, read, or modify any other QuickBooks data beyond what is required to create invoices. You can revoke access at any time from your QuickBooks account settings.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal or business data with third parties, except:

  • Supabase (our database provider) — for data storage only
  • Intuit QuickBooks — only when you explicitly request invoice creation
  • Netlify — for application hosting

6. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

7. Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information, including encrypted storage, secure HTTPS connections, and role-based access controls within the application.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data stored in FKC Logistics. To exercise these rights, contact us using the information below.

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of significant changes by updating the effective date at the top of this page.
